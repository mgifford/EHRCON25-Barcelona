# Pesca

Category: food  
Neighborhood: Jordaan  
Address: Rozengracht 133, 1016 LV Amsterdam
Link: https://www.google.com/maps?q=Pesca+Seafood+Restaurant+Amsterdam,+Rozengracht+133,+1016+LV+Amsterdam,+Netherlands
